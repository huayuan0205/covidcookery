Covidcookery

As a result of the COVID-19 pandemic, more and more people are eating meals at home. Homecooking has become as a way to pass hours and feel productive, a form to entertain ourselves, and even an outlet to channel all the anxieties. The vast majority of people have altered their food habits and eating diets. In a subReddit Covidcookery, people are posting their culinary masterpieces while they wait out the global pandemic.
